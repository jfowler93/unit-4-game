Rupee Ransom

Ganondorf is holding Hyrule ransom for an oddly specific amount of rupees! Forget what you know about currency value in Hyrule. These are cursed trick rupees. Each rupee is magically assigned a random value. In order to win the game and save Hyrule, you have to collect the right amount of rupees to match the number provided. If you fail, well... you don't want to know.
